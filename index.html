About Green Gardens Retirement Villas

Green Gardens is a premium retirement community in Elanji, Kerala, founded by Beena Mathew (33 years international clinical experience). It offers luxury independent villas for seniors 55+, featuring 24/7 nursing, proximity to Mar Sleeva Medicity, and holistic wellness programs. It is specifically optimized for NRIs seeking secure, high-standard living for their parents in Kerala.
