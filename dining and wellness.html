Dining & Wellness

Nourishment for the body.
Calm for the soul.

At Green Gardens, what you eat and how you move matter as much as where you live. Every meal is made with care. Every wellness moment is yours to choose.

"A good meal is the oldest form of love. We haven't forgotten that."
Dining
01 The Live Restaurant

Food that feels like it was made just for you — because it was.

Our multi-cuisine kitchen serves three meals a day from a rotating menu that residents help shape. Every dish is familiar, nutritious, and freshly prepared. Prefer to eat in your villa? That's always an option.

  • Daily rotating menu — Kerala home cooking & multi-cuisine
  • Meals customised for dietary needs — diabetic, low-salt, soft
  • Spacious dining area — warm, social, unhurried
  • In-villa meal delivery when preferred
"Movement that feels good today makes tomorrow easier."
Wellness
02 Yoga & Active Living

Movement that nourishes, not exhausts.

Morning yoga in the garden. A gentle pool session before lunch. A wellness class that leaves you lighter. Our activity programme was designed to invite — never to pressure. Active living is the default here; rest is always equally respected.

  • Daily yoga sessions — beginner-friendly, garden setting
  • Swimming pool — therapeutic & recreational
  • Motivation & wellness classes — group and individual
  • Garden walks on landscaped pathways
"Rest is not laziness — it's the most productive thing a body can do."
Spa & Rest
03 Beauty Salon & Spa

Looking after yourself is never an indulgence here.

The beauty salon and spa at Green Gardens exist because dignity includes looking and feeling your best. A haircut, a massage, a quiet hour that's entirely yours — these are not extras. They are part of daily life.

  • On-campus beauty salon — haircare, grooming & more
  • Spa treatments — massage, relaxation & rejuvenation
  • Library & quiet reading nooks for gentle afternoons
  • Home theatre — films, music & evening programmes
"Health isn't managed from a distance. It's attended to, daily, with familiarity."
Allied Health
04 Care That Comes to You

Medical support that feels like attentive care, not a clinic visit.

A 24-hour nurse is always on site. A doctor visits weekly — and knows your name, your history, your preferences. Specialist coordination happens without you having to navigate it alone. Your health is tracked quietly, so you can live loudly.

  • 24-hour qualified nurse always on campus
  • Weekly doctor rounds — routine & familiar
  • Allied health coordination — physio, specialist referrals
  • Direct tie-ups with nearby hospitals for emergencies
What's on Your Plate

Three cuisines. One kitchen. Made fresh, every day.

Residents choose from a rotating menu. Dietary needs are never an afterthought.

01

Kerala Home Kitchen

The food that feels like it was cooked by someone who knows you. Familiar, nourishing, and made with the spices of home.

  • Rice, sambar & vegetable curries
  • Fish curry & traditional sides
  • Puttu, appam & idiyappam
  • Seasonal vegetables, locally sourced
  • Festival specials — Onam sadya & more
02

North Indian & Continental

For variety, for grandchildren visiting, for evenings that call for something different — our kitchen travels without leaving campus.

  • Dal, roti & paneer dishes
  • Soups, salads & mild continental
  • Sandwiches, pasta & light bites
  • Fresh juices & health drinks
  • Sunday special meals for family days
03

Therapeutic & Special Diets

Every resident's health profile informs the kitchen. Dietary management is never a burden — it's simply built in.

  • Diabetic-friendly, low-glycaemic meals
  • Low-sodium & cardiac-care menus
  • Soft & pureed diets when needed
  • High-protein options for recovery
  • Dietitian-consulted meal planning

Your Menu, Your Choice

Health-Informed Cooking

Always a Table with Company

What We Promise Your Health

Care that is present, not reactive.

Health at Green Gardens is monitored quietly, so you never feel watched — only looked after.

24-Hour Nursing

A qualified nurse is always on campus. Day or night, care is never more than a call away — and the call is often made before you need to make it.

Weekly Doctor Rounds

Regular doctor visits keep your health tracked calmly. No hospital trips for routine matters — the doctor comes to you, and remembers you.

Allied Health Coordination

Physiotherapy, dietitian support, specialist referrals — we coordinate all of it, so navigating the health system never falls on your shoulders alone.

Emergency Response

Intercoms, alarm systems, and direct hospital tie-ups mean help arrives fast when it matters most. You will never be alone in an emergency.
