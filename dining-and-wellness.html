Food That Feels Like Home

At Green Gardens, dining is never an afterthought. Our multi-cuisine kitchen prepares three wholesome, flavourful meals every day — crafted around each resident's preferences, dietary requirements, and cherished tastes from home.

From the soft warmth of a morning Kerala breakfast to a leisurely lunch in our spacious dining area, every meal is an occasion to savour, connect, and enjoy life fully.

Our Approach to Food

Nutrition Meets Love

Every plate we serve is guided by one simple principle: residents should feel genuinely cared for. Our kitchen team works with a consultant nutritionist to design meals that are not only delicious, but deeply supportive of senior health.

We understand that food is deeply emotional. Families tell us the smell of a familiar curry, a warm bowl of rice kanji, or a perfectly soft chappati brings comfort that goes far beyond nutrition. We honour that.

Heart-Healthy Options

Low-sodium, low-fat preparations for residents managing blood pressure or cardiac conditions.

Diabetic-Friendly

Low-GI meals and sugar-free alternatives thoughtfully included in every menu cycle.

Fresh Ingredients

Produce sourced locally from Elanji and neighbouring farms — always seasonal, always fresh.

Family Favourites

Residents share their recipes. Our kitchen learns them, preserves them, and prepares them with care.

Our Programmes

Every Day is a Wellness Day

We offer a range of curated wellness activities designed to keep mind, body, and spirit in harmony — led by qualified instructors and healthcare professionals.

01.

Morning Yoga

Gentle asana and pranayama sessions at sunrise, tailored for seniors. Improves flexibility, balance, and mental calm.

02.

Motivation & Mindfulness

Weekly group sessions on emotional well-being, positivity, and stress management guided by a wellness counsellor.

03.

Lifestyle & Group Activities

Board games, music evenings, craft sessions, cultural celebrations and gardening clubs — always something to look forward to.

04.

Allied Health Support

Physiotherapy, occupational therapy coordination, and allied health services arranged on-site or through our hospital tie-ups.

Your Day, Your Rhythm

A Typical Day at Green Gardens

Every day here is structured with care but filled with freedom. Here's how a resident's day might flow — nourishing from morning to evening.

6:00 – 7:00 AM

Morning Yoga & Walk

Begin with gentle yoga in the garden, morning walks on landscaped paths, and optional meditation.

7:30 – 9:30 AM

Breakfast

A warm, freshly prepared breakfast served in the dining area or your own villa — your choice each morning.

10:00 AM – 12:00 PM

Activities & Engagement

Join library time, music sessions, craft activities, or simply relax in the garden lounge with good company.

12:30 – 2:00 PM

Lunch & Rest

A wholesome lunch followed by peaceful rest time. Afternoon nap is always welcome — no clocks, no rush.

4:30 PM

Evening Tea & Snacks

Gather at the clubhouse for tea, snacks, and easy conversation. One of the most beloved parts of the day.

7:00 PM

Dinner & Evening

A light, nourishing dinner followed by optional movie nights, cultural evenings, or quiet time in your villa.
