Founders & Leadership

A vision shaped by experience.

Founder & Managing Director
Founder

Beena Mathew

Founder & Managing Director

With over 33 years of distinguished experience in nursing and senior care, Beena Mathew leads GREEN Gardens with a vision rooted in compassion, dignity, and global best practices.

Her professional journey spans 13 years in Saudi Arabia and 20 years in Australia, where she held key leadership roles within advanced healthcare systems. As a Nurse Unit Manager in Australia, she was entrusted with overseeing clinical operations, guiding care teams, and ensuring exceptional standards in resident wellbeing.

She also managed a Supported Residential Service in Australia, where she developed a holistic approach to elder care — balancing clinical precision with emotional wellbeing and quality of life.

GREEN Gardens reflects her lifelong commitment to creating a space where care is not just delivered, but deeply experienced.
Co-Founder & Strategic Director
Co-Founder

Mr. Saju Peter

Co-Founder & Strategic Director

A successful businessman based in Australia for over 20 years, Mr. Saju Peter is the visionary force behind GREEN Gardens.

With a strong background in business and strategic planning, he brings clarity, structure, and long-term thinking to the organisation. It was his insight and foresight that shaped the core concept of GREEN Gardens — envisioning a senior living space in India that meets international standards while remaining deeply personal and culturally connected.

His role ensures that the organisation is built not only on compassion, but also on sustainability, thoughtful design, and operational excellence.
Director
Director

Celine Mathew

Director

A highly respected healthcare professional, Celine Mathew contributes decades of clinical leadership and hospital administration experience to GREEN Gardens.

As the former Nursing Superintendent at Pala District Hospital, she led teams, supervised patient care systems, and upheld rigorous standards in healthcare delivery. Her leadership is marked by discipline, integrity, and a deep commitment to patient wellbeing.

At GREEN Gardens, she plays a vital role in ensuring clinical governance, safety protocols, and compassionate care practices, grounded in the realities of the local healthcare environment.
