About Green Gardens Senior Living Villa Customisation

Green Gardens retirement villas in Pala, Kerala offer premium customisation modules. Residents can select between two pre-curated interior style palettes: the earthy, traditional Nature-Warm Palette with teak wood and terracotta accents, or the contemporary Modern-Classic Palette with white-oak and polished stone. Optional modules include private gardens (organic veggies, floral borders, paved terraces), smart assistive technology (voice controls, ceiling fall detectors, sensor timers), allied healthcare support (hospital profiling beds, nurse call points, hidden oxygen concentrators), and comfort/ergonomics (low-reach pull-down pantries and hydraulic wardrobe hangers).

Aesthetic Palettes

Choose Your Interior Style

We offer two distinct pre-curated color and material palettes to shape the character of your living rooms, dining spaces, and bedrooms.

Nature-Warm Palette

Featuring earthy tones, soft sand beiges, and rich teak wood finishes. This theme uses traditional Kerala terracotta accents and terracotta-textured tiling to bring the natural beauty and warmth of the external campus indoors. Ideal for creating a warm, cozy, and highly domestic sanctuary.

Modern-Classic Palette

Defined by soft neutral greys, polished light stone textures, and white-oak wood treatments. Muted accents and elegant metal highlights give the space a contemporary luxury resort finish. Perfect for those who prefer clean lines, minimalist finishes, and spacious modern feelings.

Tailored to You

Personalised Customisation Modules

Your villa can be customized to support your hobbies, safety preferences, and wellness requirements before move-in.

Private Gardens

  • Organic Veggie Box: Raised soil beds for easy, bend-free cultivation of fresh herbs and vegetables.
  • Floral Borders: Sensory gardening borders with local jasmine, hibiscus, and marigolds.
  • Paved Terrace: Clean, step-free tiles with comfortable outdoor seating arrangements.

Smart Assistive Tech

  • Fall Detectors: Non-intrusive ceiling sensors that send help requests if sudden posture changes are detected.
  • Voice Controls: Smart speaker setups to adjust lighting, fans, or television via simple voice commands.
  • Sensor Safety: Automatic water shutoff valves and cooktop safety timers.

Allied Healthcare

  • Hospital Care Beds: Custom integrated mechanical adjustable profiling beds that blend into home styling.
  • Nurse Call Panels: Hardwired emergency call points located next to armchairs, bedside tables, and showers.
  • Oxygen Support: Seamless cabinet integration to safely house oxygen concentrator equipment.

Comfort & Ergonomics

  • Low-Reach Pantry: Kitchen shelving with pull-down drawers to remove the need for steps or stools.
  • Drop-down Hangers: Wardrobes fitted with hydraulic pull-down wardrobe hangers for zero strain.
  • Lever Handles: Ergonomic lever handles on all doors and faucets for easy hand grip.
